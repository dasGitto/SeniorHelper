Chicken Souvlaki

with Lemon Rice

The Basics

  • Chicken (Breasts or Thighs)
  • Rice (Long grain or Basmati)
  • Olive Oil & Lemon
  • Garlic & Oregano
  • Pita Bread & Tzatziki

Tap "Start" to begin! ↓

1

Prep the Chicken

Cut the chicken into large, even pieces. Put them in a bowl.

2

Make Marinade

Mix 1/4 cup olive oil, juice of 1 lemon, 2 cloves garlic, and lots of oregano.

3

Marinate

Pour marinade over chicken. Let it sit for 30 minutes in the fridge.

4

Cook the Rice

Boil rice with a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lemon until fluffy.

5

Grill

Thread chicken on sticks. Grill for 10 minutes until golden brown.

6

Finished!

Serve the chicken over a big bed of rice with warm pita bread. Enjoy!
